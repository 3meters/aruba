Aircandi Invitation

We are excited to welcome you to Aircandi, a mobile application that helps you do more with the places you love!

  • Connect with places faster: Proximity features can discover and connect to places faster inside or out.
  • See the big picture: Lots of great sources like Foursquare, Facebook, Twitter, and Yelp are organized for easy access.
  • Make places better: Customize and extend places to make them better for everyone.
  • Share new places: Create and share new places that would love to be discovered.

To get Aircandi and create your Aircandi account, click to the Google Play store below. If you have questions, we really want to hear from you! Use our aircandi forum , email us at info@3meter.com , or use the feedback feature in Aircandi.
